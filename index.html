about

A bit about me

I'm Geofrey Waithaka, a frontend developer focused on building web interfaces that are clean, fast, and easy to use. I care about the details that most people don't notice — the spacing, the load time, the way a button feels when you click it.

I work mainly with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, building responsive layouts and interactive interfaces that work well across devices. I enjoy turning an idea or a design into something people can actually click through and use.

I'm currently looking to take on more projects and grow as a developer — if you've got something in mind, I'd like to hear about it.
